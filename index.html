College Course Tracker

The College Course Tracker is a powerful, user-friendly, and secure application designed to simplify academic planning and organization. Whether you're managing terms, tracking course details, or scheduling assessments, this app helps you stay on top of your academic progress with ease.

Key Features

  • Term Management: Add, edit, and delete academic terms, including start and end dates.
  • Course Tracking: Manage up to six courses per term with essential details like course name, status, instructor information, notes, and key dates.
  • Assessment Management: Track objective and performance assessments with notifications for start and end dates.
  • Notes and Sharing: Add and share course notes seamlessly via email or SMS.
  • Notifications: Stay ahead of deadlines with customizable local push notifications.
  • Data Security: All data is securely stored locally using SQLite, ensuring your personal and academic information remains private and protected. No cloud storage means full control over your data.
  • Local Hosting: With SQLite, data is stored efficiently on your device, ensuring quick access even without an internet connection.

Built with .NET MAUI

The local storage architecture enhances security while providing offline access, making it ideal for students seeking a reliable and secure academic tool.
